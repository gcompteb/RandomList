# Privacy Policy for RandomList

**Last updated: February 10, 2026**

## Introduction

RandomList ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free Android application that allows users to create custom lists and pick random items from them. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we receive:
- Your Google display name
- Your Google email address
- Your Google account unique identifier

### User-Generated Content
- The lists you create (list names and items)
- These are stored in Google Firebase Firestore and associated with your account

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
- Authenticate you and provide access to the app
- Store and synchronize your lists across your devices
- Maintain the functionality of the app

We do **not**:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Track your activity outside the app
- Share your data with anyone other than the service providers listed below

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

- **Google Firebase Authentication**: To handle user sign-in. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google Firebase Firestore**: To store and sync your lists. [Firebase Terms](https://firebase.google.com/terms)

These services may collect additional technical data such as device identifiers and IP addresses as described in their respective privacy policies.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore
- All data is transmitted using encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account only
- We use Firebase security rules to ensure no other user can access your data

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data is retained as long as you have an active account
- You can delete individual lists at any time within the app
- To delete all your data, you can:
  - Delete all your lists within the app
  - Contact us at the email below to request full account data deletion

## Children's Privacy

RandomList is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
